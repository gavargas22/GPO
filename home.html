We are a business and engineering student organization
We develop, create, promote, and manage cutting edge engineering projects with one goal. To bring a unique experience to the members in the form of an engineering company.

We are a group of students from the colleges of Engineering and Business Administration; we aim to prepare our members for the 21st century world, by giving them the ideal of the love of learning and the passionate pursuit of skill and imagination.
Open Source Mission!

We aspire to foster interdisciplinary fellowship and assistance amongst university clubs and majors. We intend to facilitate the exchange of information and provide an open forum through which clubs can request help on different issues from other organizations who may have more expertise in a given area.

Our projects are accomplished in an open source and open learning environment where every member has the possibility to work on various subjects and learn from a multidisciplinary nature. Our projects relate to propulsion systems and comprise a broad spectrum of disciplines.
